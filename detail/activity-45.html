    IMG Worlds of Adventure

    Admission ticket included

    Secure your entry in advance on a date good for you with this all-day pass to Dubai’s IMG Worlds of Adventure. IMG Worlds of Adventure is one of Dubai’s most popular theme parks that offers visitors a range of activities and attractions. The ticket includes access to all of the attractions in the park, as well as unlimited rides on all roller coasters and other attractions inside park. This is an all-day pass that lets you enjoy IMG Worlds of Adventure. It is a great way to spend your holiday without having to worry about finding something else to do in Dubai. The prebooking Skip-the-line process gives visitors a chance to enjoy the anticipation of visiting a new destination before they arrive. This allows them to get a taste of what they can expect from their visit and allows them to ride all rides inside mega-entertainment park. The Ticket includes with: - IMG World of adventures admission ticket to enjoy the unlimited rides inside park. - All Rides and Attraction - Free Parking
