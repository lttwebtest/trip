  • What you can expect

    Embark on an exhilarating desert adventure on this safari along the red-gold dunes of Al Labab. Enjoy the thrill of dune bashing and then immerse yourself in local culture at a desert camp, where you can see Tanoura dancing and sip Arabic coffee, and visit local markets.

    Get picked up from the location of your choice in a Land Cruiser and then driven to the Red Dunes of Al Labab, approximately a 45-minute journey. Here, admire the majestic dunes and the wonderful colors that change throughout the day.

    Then spend an adventurous 20-30 minutes of hard dune bashing before heading further across the dunes to reach the desert camp.

    At the camp, experience a short camel ride, traditional henna hand decoration of the East, and a show of traditional, belly, and Tanoura dancing. Other activities include quad biking, a sand art display, Arabic coffee, falcon photography, and a carpet stall. After, return to your location of choi
