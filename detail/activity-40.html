  • What you can expect

    Experience a day out the entire family can enjoy at Ferrari World. Discover thrilling roller coasters, live shows, interactive games, and displays. There's something for everyone at this thrilling theme park in Dubai.

    Sit back and enjoy your ride through the desert as you journey to Abu Dhabi and the thrilling attractions of Ferrari World—not only the world's first Ferrari-themed park, but also the world's largest indoor amusement space. The familiar brand of the prancing horse is not known for its subtlety; see how the passion and innovation of their cars is recreated in a fun format for all to enjoy.

    Fly up to speeds of 150 miles per hour (240 km/h) on one of the world's fastest rollercoasters, the Formula Rossa. Feel what it's like to be a Formula 1 driver as you accelerate to 62 miles per hour (100 km/h) in just 2 seconds, experiencing 1.7 G of force. There are also plenty of less adrenaline-fueled—but no less interesting—activities to enjoy, including arcade games and live shows.
