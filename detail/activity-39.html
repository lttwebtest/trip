  • Know before you book

    - Children 2 and younger are complimentary.

    - Child rate applies for ages 3 to 7 that are below 1.2 meters in height.

    - Some attractions may have height restrictions.

    - Hours of operation at Aquaventure are 10 AM–sunset. However hours of operation vary; please check before you go.

    - Redemption of voucher is on the selected event date; amendments to the date are not allowed.

    - In order to redeem your voucher, you must present a valid passport or government-issued photo ID that matches the contact name you used when booking. If you do not present a valid form of identification, you will not receive admission, and you will not be issued a refund.

    - Locker and Towel rentals are available within the waterpark.

    - You may bring along your own towels and water bottles. However the water bottles are limited to a maximum 2 small 500 ml per person.

  • What you can expect

    The Trident Has Risen!

    We’re making waves as the largest waterpark in the world with the launch of Trident Tower, Splashers Lagoon and Splashers Cove. Home to record-breaking slides and first-in-the-region experiences, Trident Tower, Splashers Lagoon and Splashers Cove open now!

    Important Update:Ahead of becoming the world’s leading aquatic theme park, 2021 will be a transformative year for Atlantis Aquaventure as the region’s best-loved waterpark embarks on a year-long journey of innovation. With this in mind, from the 1st March, the Tower of Neptune, Splashers Mountain, Splashers Island, Torrent Beach and The River Rapids will be closed to undergo enhancements.

    Start by blasting off on water coasters and plummeting down a 9-story, near-vertical slide straight into a shark-filled lagoon. Take it down a notch and soak up some sun as you glide effortlessly along a lazy river ride, or simply kick back with a book on the beautiful private beach.
