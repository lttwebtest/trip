  • Know before you book

    - Children 2 and younger are complimentary.

    - Some attractions may have height restrictions.

    - Thursday evening is Ladies' Night; admission is only available to women and girls as well as boys under the age of 8. Valid ID may be required as proof of age for boys.

    - Select an adult ticket if you are 3 feet 5 inches (106 cm) or taller, otherwise select a child ticket.

    - In order to redeem your voucher, you must present a valid passport or government-issued photo ID that matches the contact name you used when booking. If you do not present a valid form of identification, you will not receive admission, and you will not be issued a refund.

    - You may carry your own set of towels to save some money.

    - Outside food is not permitted to taken inside. There is a strict checking of all the bags.

    - Locker rentals are available and will need to be paid directly to the park at the entrance counter. The published rates are Small AED 48, Medium AED 69, Large AED 90.

    - The published rates is subject to change without any prior notice

  • What you can expect

    Splash into the Wild Wadi Waterpark and discover a fun-soaked playground based on the tale of Juha, a popular character in Arabian folklore. Take a day out from the bustle of the city to explore more than 30 rides and attractions, including some of the most thrilling waterslides on the planet.

    If you’re the adventurous type, head straight to the Jumeirah Sceirah. Hold on to your swimsuit as you ride over 3 drops, reaching speeds of up to 50 miles per hour (80 km/h). Master Blasters take you on an exhilarating water roller coaster ride as you shoot up and down interconnected slides. For those thrill seekers amongst you, check out the adrenaline fuelled rides including the incredible Tantrum Alley and Burj Surj!

    Last but by no means least, our Wipeout and Riptide Flowriders offer the ultimate surfing experience. One of the only four such rides in the world, the Wipeout Flowrider is a blast.

    For the little ones, there is endless fun with plenty of slides, games, and water cannons in their own area—Juha's Dhow and Lagoon—leaving you to kick back and relax under the hot desert sun, venture to the Breaker's Bay wave pool, or enjoy a tranquil ride on the Juha's Journey lazy river ride.
