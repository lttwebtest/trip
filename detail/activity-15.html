  • What you can expect

    Uncover the natural wonders of the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve, a facility committed to preserving the biodiversity of the UAE. From large mammals to a stunning variety of birdlife and plants, discover the life within the desert from the opulence of an upscale Range Rover 4x4.

    Just a few miles away from the glitz and glamor of downtown Dubai is the serene and peaceful Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve. Roll over the dunes with ease as your driver navigates windswept sands in a comfortably equipped Range Rover. With the help of high quality binoculars, keep your eyes peeled for local wildlife like elegant gazelles, pet-sized but ferocious Gordon's wildcats, desert monitor lizards, and the biggest local success story—the white Arabian oryx, which was saved from extinction and reintroduced into the wild in the last 30 years.

    Take a moment to appreciate the valuable conservation work this reserve performs in the fascinating desert ecosystem of the UAE. Your day will conclude with a fresh juice, tea/coffee, and a delicious breakfast at the famous Al Maha Desert Resort.
