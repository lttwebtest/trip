  • What you can expect

    Experience Dubai’s best attractions while saving up to 50 percent on general admission entry with the Dubai Pass.

    Enjoy breathtaking views from the observation deck of the world’s tallest building, Burj Khalifa, explore the Dubai surrounds on a 4x4 Desert Safari, be amazed at the Motiongate theme park, or visit the extraordinary Dubai Aquarium.

    Valid for 7 days from the date of first use, the Dubai Pass offers you unbeatable value, convenience and flexibility. Choose from entry to 3, 5 or 7 top Dubai attractions for one single price, and plan your days to suit your interests and schedule.

    The capital of the United Arab Emirates (UAE), Dubai is an extraordinary city known for its luxury shopping, adventure and entertainment attractions, and ultra-modern skyscrapers.

    Indeed, at 830m, Burj Khalifa is the world’s tallest building, dominating an already impressive skyline. A trip up to the observation deck of Burj Khalifa is an absolute must, where you’ll be in awe of the incredible views over Dubai city and beyond.

    Whatever your tastes and interests, the Dubai Pass is your ticket to explore Dubai and its surrounds as conveniently and cost effectively as possible.
