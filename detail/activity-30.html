  • Know before you book

    - Children 17 and younger are not allowed.

    - If you are pregnant, have limited mobility, or fear heights and water, you should not take part.

    - You must weigh between 100 lbs and 330 lbs pounds (46–150kg).

    - You should have sufficient travel, injury, sickness, or accident (including sporting accidents) insurance.

    - Check-in is 15 minutes prior to the booked tour start time.

    - You must know how to swim

  • What you can expect

    Get ready for a totally innovative water adventure as you strap a JetBlade water jet pack onto your feet and prepare to fly over the Dubai waterfront. A thrilling 30-minute flight allows you to practice different techniques and movements as you speed, swirl, and soar above the waves.

    Flyboarding is the newest aquatic activity for thrill-seeking adrenaline-lovers looking for a watery rush. An experienced instructor gives you a safety briefing and a rundown on how the equipment works—this high-powered jetpack propels water downward, shooting you up into the air or letting you speed beneath the surface, dolphin-style.

    Once you're comfortable and have some tips and techniques down, it's time to fly. The JetBlade connects to a jet ski for power, and then you're off. Fly, dive, twist, and flip through the air and under the waves—a coach is always nearby to offer guidance or suggestions for enhancing the fun as you master your balance and hone your skill at controlling your elevation, speed, and angle on this amazing piece of equipment.
