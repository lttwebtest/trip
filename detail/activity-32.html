  • What you can expect

    This Includes an Exclusive Doorstep transfer from anywhere in Dubai, Sharjah and Ajman for VIP Desert Safari. Dune bashing in the red sands of the Lehbab desert. Guests will be welcomed with traditional Arabic coffee (Gahwa) and tea on arrival at the campsite. Also experiencing Camel riding, Henna painting and Other Entertainment such as Belly Dance, Fire Dance & Tanoura Show. Arabic costumes will be on-hand for a self-photograph, an International grand buffet dinner with BBQ and Vegetarian foods available in options. Arabic sweets, fruits, unlimited soft drinks and water are offered throughout the Tour.

    Our VIP Lounge will let you feel Special on the Spot. Personally assisted Bedouin staffs will serve you & your guest in a traditional way of welcome. An Exclusive Mini Buffet Dinner will be served.
