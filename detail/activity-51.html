  • Know before you book

    - Both routes depart from Dubai Mall Bus Station (although you can start the tour at any stop) - first tour departure is 10:00am and last tour departure is 16:00 on both routes. Duration - Red Route: 80 minutes, Blue Route: 130 minutes. Frequency - every 30 minutes (Red), 60 minutes (Blue)

    - Sunset & Night Tour Departs at 17:00pm from Dubai Mall. Please note this is not a hop-on hop-off route and last 2 hours approx.

    - Audio tour commentary languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic.

    - Mobile and paper vouchers are accepted for this tour and are redeemable at any stop along the route.

    - Vouchers are valid for 12 months giving greater flexibility if your plans change.

    - Cruise connector route only operates on cruise arrival days and will drop off/pick-up at Dubai Mall. Operational times vary depending on cruise ship dis-embarkation times, check locally for latest information.

  • What you can expect

    Discover the very best of Dubai on an unlimited hop-on hop-off bus tour. Explore this mesmerising destination on your own schedule, seeing all of the key landmarks along the way, including the Burj Khalifa, Burj Al Arab, Dubai Marina City, Palm Jumeirah, the Dubai Mall, the Spice & Gold Souk and much more. With access to 2 main routes and 19 stops, you'll be sure to see every aspect of Dubai on your sightseeing journey. Enjoy stunning 360-degree views from the open-top deck, and hear fascinating historical insights thanks to the on-board audio tour commentary.—
