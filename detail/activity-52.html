  • Know before you book

  • What you can expect

    Dubai, being surrounded by water, is home to a few water channels that extend throughout the city. This dinner cruise will sail on the Creek side of old Dubai.

    We'll offer this dinner cruise on a wonderful wooden dhow built in the time-honored manner, channeling the region's historical heritage and traditions, yet with the utmost comfort that you can expect in a present day ship. Spend a romantic evening between the glistening stars as you watch the waves dance in the night while you cruise along the moonlit creek.

    Enjoy a sumptuous buffet dinner served with a taste of local delicacies as you sail through the deep blue Creek.

    Pick up and drop off from your hotel is included in a sharing coach.
