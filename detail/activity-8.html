  • Know before you book

    - Desert Safari is not recommended for pregnant ladies, guests with back, spine or heart health issues, guests with special needs

    - Pick up for Desert safari is available from Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman between 03:00 pm - 03:30 pm.

    - You will receive an electronic copy of your Burj Khalifa tickets to print along with your exact timed-entry time for the Burj Khalifa after your booking is confirmed.

    - In order to redeem your voucher, you must present a valid passport or photo ID that matches the contact name you used when booking. If you do not present a valid form of identification, you will not receive admission, and you will not be issued a refund.

    - On the 31st Dec, with regards to the Desert Safari Tour, there will be no pick & drop from The Palm island hotels and Business Bay area due to the New Year's Eve Fireworks preparation, the areas will be closed from afternoon onwards.

    - Not recommended for children below 3 y.o.

  • What you can expect

    Combine 2 of Dubai’s most popular experiences as you sample desert life in an authentic camp and enjoy views from the world’s tallest building. This package celebrates the old and new of Dubai with a fun-filled evening on the dunes as well as sky-high views amid the cutting-edge, modern architecture at Burj Khalifa.

    4x4 Desert Safari:Head to the rolling dunes of the Emirati desert. Heading off the paved roads, the 4x4 vehicle eases over the challenging terrain. In the distance, you can see the red glow of the setting sun as it drops behind the Hajar Mountains. There’s plenty of time to try your hand at the thrilling activity of sandboarding, or you can get into the spirit of nomadic life with a camel ride before you make your way to camp.

    The appetizing aromas of the barbecue are enough to get your mouth watering. Indulge in freshly grilled meat served with dates and Arabian coffee. Relax and enjoy complimentary soft drinks with a shisha or some henna painting. Belly dancers put on an entertaining show around the campfire before the evening concludes and you return to your hotel.

    Burj Khalifa AtTheTop: Make your way to Downtown Dubai, where the world’s tallest building soars more than 2,700 feet (830 m) in the sky. Aboard the high-speed elevators, ascend to the 124th floor and stroll around the observation deck, admiring 360-degree views of the futuristic city with all its famous landmarks, including Burj al Arab, The Palm, and more.
