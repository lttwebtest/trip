  • What you can expect

    Get splashin’ at the largest waterpark in the world with the launch of Trident Tower, Splashers Lagoon and Splashers Cove, NOW open with 28 new record-breaking waterslides and attractions. Aquaventure Waterpark promises a world of excitement to make you feel #DifferentInWater in more ways than one. Get your pulse racing with 79 slides and incredible attractions, make your way through the Raging Rapids and relax on 1km of private beach. Young families will enjoy the safety of Splashers’ Kids Play Area, a dream attraction for families for non-stop splashin’ FUN, while animal lovers can get up close with sea lions, sharks and stingrays. Enjoy over 22 fun-filled hectares of record-breaking fun for all ages.

    Important Information: Ahead of becoming the world’s leading aquatic theme park and largest waterpark in the world, 2021 will be a transformative year for Atlantis Aquaventure as the region’s best-loved waterpark embarks on a year-long journey of innovation. With this in mind, from the 1st March, the Tower of Neptune, Splashers Mountain, Splashers Island, Surfs Up, Torrent Beach and The Rapids will be closed to undergo enhancements.

    Dry off and continue the watery fun as you explore The Lost Chambers Aquarium, where you can come face to face with more than 65,000 marine creatures. Explore the mystery of the lost city of Atlantis through 20 unique exhibits, including a touch tank and Aquatheatre interactive show.
