  • What you can expect

    The First 3D Trick Art Museum in Dubai!

    Over a 185 3D reality-defying artworks for selfies– featuring YOU!

    3D World is a 23,000 sq. ft museum where you can indulge your creativity and imagination.

    Fun times. Memorable pictures. Explore our interactive gallery and have a blast!

    Trick-eye really lives up to its name of 'tricking your eyes'. These are creative artworks where people can interact with the art.

    There's a lot of posing, climbing, crawling, sitting to do - the whole experience is a ton of fun.

    3D World Dubai brings you closer to art through the use of ‘Trick-Art’ paintings and optical illusions.

    Each artwork is carefully chosen and painted by hand by our team of talented and passionate artists; all artists are master carfts men excelling in their craft. There's a whole lot art to indulge in but it will work best if you let your imagination run wild to take the best pictures. However, if you're really not into imagining stuff don't worry, our staff will gladly help you come up with ideas that will create memories for you to take home

    Immerse yourself in our 3-Dimensional settings in the nine different zones at 3D World Dubai!

    “Be a part of the Art” in our paintings and create memorable photos of your own with your creativity and imagination.

    At 3D World Dubai, we encourage visitors to interact and have fun with the art, taking as many photos as you want. These 3D paintings are not complete if you are not in them!

    Fun Begins HERE!

    If you are ever in Dubai, we encourage you to visit this awesome museum and be a part of the 3D Paintings. It is the brainchild of a husband and wife Raman Ticku and Shakun Singh, who were first inspired by a trick-art museum in the Phuket on a family vacation and have been building the idea ever since.
