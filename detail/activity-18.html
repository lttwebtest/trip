  • What you can expect

    Explore Abu Dhabi in an English guided tour, also audio guided in French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Spanish, and Chinese with free wifi on board.

    Participate in Gray line's ultimate Abu Dhabi full day tour and take advantage of an ideal introduction to the largest city of the Emirates, which also happens to be the UAE capital. Abu Dhabi is an Island, and its name translates into: “Father of the Gazelle”. Our first stop will be at the Sheikh Zayed Grand mosque, one of the largest and most beautiful Mosques in the world; able to accommodate an impressive number of over 30,000 worshipers. The exquisite white structure is completely clad in marble, featuring intricate Islamic art and carvings, as well as extensive landscaping.

    After this moving experience, we will continue our tour towards the YAS island and its Red Ferrari largest covered park, before we stop at the the Yas Mall. We will enjoy the biggest mall of Abu Dhabi and we'll have lunch before we continue our tour .

    The remaining part of the tour will be devoted to a drive through the famous giant Saadiyat Island project where the Louvre and Guggenheim museums are being built to complete the image of Abu Dhabi as a cultural Arab capital before entering the fruit and dates market in Abu Dhabi harbor.

    We will then drive along the spectacular Corniche, past a beautiful paradoxical collection of gleaming white mosques, glass-clad offices, the Emirates Palace and its opulence, the palace of the Emir and elegant high-rise apartments to the tip of the breakwater for a superb view of the city’s magnificent skyline. We'll stop next at the Heritage Village, which houses a small museum that depicts the traditional daily activities of the Arab community prior to the discovery of oil.

    At the end of the afternoon, we will head up back to Dubai passing again through the Yas island before we reach Dubai after approximately 1h30 drive.
