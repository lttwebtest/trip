  • What you can expect

    Embark on a morning or evening desert safari across the Al Lahbab sand dunes from Dubai. Choose from a variety of activities, from sandboarding to quad biking, to a BBQ dinner in the desert.

    tart your day with hotel pickup in a 4WD vehicle and drive out to the desert with your safari guide. Choose from 4-hour and 7-hour tour options. Take a 25-minute break and enjoy an optional quad bike experience. Then, take a 30-45 minute dune drive across the desert in a 4x4 vehicle. Admire beautiful 360-degree views of the desert landscape as the sun slowly sets. Try sandboarding, before heading to the desert camp where the 4-hour tour ends. Then, relax with return service to your hotel.

    The 7-hour option continues with a visit to a hidden desert camp. Enjoy an Arabic coffee, dates, and Lugamat, a traditional sweet, upon arrival. Hop on a camel for a short ride, decorate your hands with Henna tattoos, or take pictures.

    Enjoy a BBQ dinner from your private table. Taste kebabs, hummus, and salads with sodas, water, and drinks from the bar. Take in a belly dancing show and Tanura performance. Snap some photos with the Arabian falcons before returning to your hotel in Dubai.
