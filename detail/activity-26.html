  • Activity itinerary

    La Perle DXB

    Admission ticket included

    La Perle by Dragone is the region’s first permanent show, featuring 65 world-class artists. Created by world-renowned Artistic Director Franco Dragone and brought to Dubai by Al Habtoor Group, La Perle combines the spirit of Dubai with an iconic aqua-stage to deliver an unmissable spectacle in, on and above water. La Perle is located in the heart of Dubai at Al Habtoor City and is easily accessible from all areas in Dubai. Surrounded by three five-star hotels, Habtoor Palace, LXR Hotels and Resorts, V Hotel, Curio Collection by Hilton and Hilton Dubai Al Habtoor City. La Perle offers easy access to sensational food and beverage options that are all within walking distance of the theater. Choose between a magnificent variety of more than 20 restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues. For more information, please reach out to the box office team Operation hours: Sunday & Monday: 10 am – 6 pm Tuesday – Saturday: 10 am – 11 pm
