  • What you can expect

    Burj Khalifa is the world's tallest skyscraper, built with a combination of limitless visionary mindset and solid science. Standing higher than 2,716 feet (828 m), the tower amasses awe-inspiring facts, figures, and statistics. Here's your chance to see Dubai from atop this remarkable building.

    When you arrive, take a high-speed lift up the tower and be greeted by a spacious observation deck outfitted with large viewing windows. Burj Khalifa holds records as the world's tallest building and free-standing structure, the world's highest occupied floor, and highest observation deck, among others, and the sheer immensity of this extraordinary architectural achievement is sure to take your breath away.

    At the observation deck, enjoy bird's-eye views of Dubai and its many other impressive buildings. Catch glimpses of golden desert sands in the distance and sparkling ocean stretching out as far as the eye can see. Have your camera ready—you're going to want to relive these 360-degree panoramic views. End your experience with a fast and easy ride back down to earth in the world-class elevator.
