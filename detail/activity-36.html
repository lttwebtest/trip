  • Activity itinerary

    Lahbab

    3h

    one AC 4WD vehicle. After around an hour’s journey, you will reach the desert. There you will get some time at a restroom for relaxing / warming up, while your vehicle is being equipped for a desert ride with suitable tire pressure and other adjustments. You may choose to ride a Quad bike in surroundings as an optional extra. The safari begins with dune bashing. After enjoying about 15-20 minutes of dune bashing, thrill yourselves further by trying sandboarding (sand surfing) & Nice spot for Pictures, Next, we will proceed to ‘authentic desert camp.

    Desert Tours Dubai

    3h

    Admission ticket included

    At the desert camp, get a welcome in a typical way with Dates, Sweets, and Qahwa (Arabic Coffee). Feel the real Bedouin encampment as you sit in open air or Majlis - cushions and low tables to sit on with carpets spread all over. Near the camp, take a ride in a Camel caravan/group. You may also witness the sunset view (subject to the situation, not guaranteed) from the sunset dune, close to Camp. Back at Camp, enjoy smoking Shisha, try Henna painting. Also do an Arab makeover in local costumes and take few snaps with one Falcon resting on your hand or shoulder. Soon you can watch the colorful performance of Tanoura dance, and have a dinner open buffet that includes Mix grilled, Salads, Desserts, and all (Veg & Non Veg Options Both Available. In addition, the Ladies Khaliji Dance (belly Dance) & Fire Show will keep you entertained. At last, you will be returned to the select location or hotel.
