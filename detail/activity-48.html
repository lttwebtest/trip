  • Know before you book

    - Travelers should have at least a moderate level of physical fitness

    - Infant 0 - 1.99 years (complimentary).Complimentary access for children two years and under upon presentation of ID.Adult rate applicable from 02 years old , no discount for children.

    - Wild Wadi reserves the right to close entry/amend operating hours without prior notice and without liability. Some of the rides in Wild Wadi have a height requirement of minimum 1.1 metres - therefore please note that guests below 1.1 metres won't be able to play on all the rides in the waterpark.

    - Some rides are not suitable for guests with disabilities, pregnant guests, guests with heart or back conditions.

    - If transfer option selected ,pick up from Dubai hotels & drop off at Wild wadi main gate.Children below 5 years old must be seated in a child seat while traveling in any kind of vehicle in UAE. If you have a child below 5 years joining the tour, please mention in the remarks.

    - Swimming attire is recommended for Wild Wadi water park. Appropriate swimwear MUST be worn at all times regardless of age. Management reserves the right to determine whether or not swimwear is appropriate.

    - Guests visiting the UAE during the Summer months (June – September) are advised that peak daytime temperatures sometimes reach 42 – 45 degrees Celsius. Humidity levels can also exceed 90 percent during this period. We recommend all our guests to take adequate precaution.

  • Activity itinerary

    Wild Wadi Waterpark

    Admission ticket included

    The Wild Wadi Water Park is one of the world’s famous water park in the UAE. The park offers the largest and the choicest collection of attractions, and facilities for adults and children for guaranteed family fun, including convenient changing rooms. Your admission ticket offers all-day access to the park’s attractions, including thrilling water slides such as the Tantrum Alley ,Burj Surj, Master Blaster, Breakers Bay, Wipeout etc. Wild Wadi waterpark offers full of attractions for fun-lovers of all ages, with numerous slides, kids pool, wave pool, restaurants and shopping choices. You can visit the Wild wadi water park on your own, or you can select the transfer option when booking to include pickup and drop-off in an air-conditioned private vehicle from Dubai hotels.
