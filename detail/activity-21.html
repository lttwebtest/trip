  • Know before you book

    - Desert Safari is not recommended for pregnant ladies, guests with back, spine or heart health issues, guests with special needs

    - Desert Safari is not recommended for children below 3 y.o.

    - Please note, during the month of Ramadan there is no entertainment and no alcohol drinks will be served

    - Pickup begins approximately 1 hour before your cruise departure time. You will confirm your exact pickup time when you call to confirm your reservation

    - You might be sharing the table with other tourists

    - Open-air upper deck/air-conditioned lower deck on Dhow

    - Tours are organised on sharing basis

    - Off road driving is included in Safari Tour

    - Both trips are in the evening. Hence they cannot be done on the same day
