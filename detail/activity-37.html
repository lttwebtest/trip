  • Know before you book

    - Children 3 and younger are not allowed. Minimum age is 4 years old.

    - If you are pregnant, you should not take part.

    - If you have back, neck, or muscular problems, you should carefully consider whether to participate.

    - Free ATV (15 minutes) at the camp only for 13 yrs and above, You do not need a driver's license to drive the ATV. You drive on your own following a guide.

    - Pickup is available from hotels in Dubai. You will be able to arrange pickup from your hotel or a nearby location when you call to confirm your reservation.

    - You will receive a mandatory temperature check

  • What you can expect

    Experience the ultimate mix of adventures in the Arabian Desert on a 4x4 desert safari . Motor over the trackless sands tackling the rolling dunes in a 4-WD, and then enjoy a sumptuous Arabic BBQ dinner complete with music and dance.

    Set off on an exhilarating off-road adventure through the beautiful expanse of the Arabian Desert as a professional driver leads you over the sands in a Jeep. Head to a fantastic lookout point to take in the full glory of the desert as the sun sinks down, and then make your way to a traditional desert camp, where a variety of entertaining activities await.

    Back at the camp, go for a ride on a gentle camel, have a henna design painted on your skin, smoke aromatic shisha from a water pipe, and snap your picture in traditional Arabic dress.

    As you enjoy the spirit of Middle Eastern hospitality, take a seat for a delectable, 3-course feast of Emirati specialties prepared before your eyes, including savory grilled meats, fresh salads, and local sweets. A swaying belly dancer sets the festive ambiance, and the soft notes of Arabian folk music fill the air as you relax beneath the stars.
